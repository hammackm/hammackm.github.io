Skin Cancer Detection Using Convolutional Neural Networks

Motivation

Skin cancers are different from other cancers in a obvious but important way. The cancers develop and appear on the skin. Special medical technologies are not needed to observe the cancer. For this reason they are a good candidate for the use of computer vision based diagnosis/recognition. Melanoma is the deadliest form of skin cancer.
